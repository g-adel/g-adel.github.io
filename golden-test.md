# Main Content Area
(Right Side)

This is the largest area in the golden ratio layout. It serves as the primary reading area.

### Proportion Math

If the total width is $W$, we split it into $a$ and $b$ such that $\frac{a+b}{a} = \frac{a}{b} = \phi \approx 1.618$.

- **Main Area Width**: $\approx 61.8\%$
- **Left Column Width**: $\approx 38.2\%$

Inside the Left Column, we have a square at the bottom. The remaining top section forms another Golden Rectangle, allowing for infinite recursion.
